When You Need More Information

You've read the evidence on Marcus's case. The pro's claim was in General Comments, the review was in Admin, the customer's profile was one click away. Most of the time, that's everything you need to decide.

But sometimes it isn't. The pro says the customer is confusing them with another pro — and nothing on the record proves or disproves that. Before you decide, you need to hear from the other party. Setting up that outreach takes two moves: create the Quick Case, then link the Thumbtack User if Salesforce doesn't do it for you.
